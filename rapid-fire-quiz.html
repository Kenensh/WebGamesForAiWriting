⚡ How to Play Rapid Fire Quiz

Objective: Answer as many questions correctly as possible in this fast-paced knowledge challenge.

🎮 How to Play:

  • 📋 Questions: Answer 20 rapid-fire questions
  • ⏱️ Time Limit: 10 seconds per question
  • 🎯 Click: Click the correct answer button
  • ⚡ Speed: Faster answers = higher score

🏆 Scoring System:

  • ✅ Correct Answer: Base points + time bonus
  • 🔥 Streak Bonus: Extra points for consecutive correct answers
  • ⏰ Time Bonus: More points for faster responses
  • ❌ Wrong Answer: No points, streak resets
  • ⏱️ Timeout: Counts as wrong answer

📚 Categories:

  • 🧠 General: Mixed knowledge topics
  • 🔬 Science: Physics, chemistry, biology
  • 📚 History: World history and events
  • ⚽ Sports: Sports facts and trivia
  • 🎬 Movies: Film and entertainment
  • 🎲 Mixed: Random questions from all categories

🔥 Special Features:

  • 🎯 Hot Streaks: Build momentum with consecutive correct answers
  • 📊 Performance Tracking: Monitor accuracy and response times
  • 🏃 Speed Recognition: Visual feedback for quick responses
  • 💫 Streak Indicators: See your hot streaks in real-time

💡 Tips:

  • Read questions quickly but carefully
  • Trust your first instinct
  • Build streaks for maximum points
  • Practice different categories to improve
